Throw the Stone

Red target
The red target has one life and gives 1 point.
Blue target
The blue target has one life, but does not give any points. Instead, hitting it gives 3 more stones.
Yellow target
The yellow target has one life, but does also not give any points. Instead, hitting it gives 10 more stones.
Green target
The green target has two lives and gives 3 points.
Timer target
The time limit for hitting the target depends on your score. If you don't hit it within these seconds, you lose 5 stones for blue targets or 5 points for red targets.
Moving target
All different targets spawn with a chance to move vertically or horizontally.
Black hole
The black hole pulls the stone toward itself. Every time a stone is sucked in, you lose 3 points and the black hole becomes bigger and stronger.
